    • PLC/DCS SOLUTION

    We offer multiple automation and control strategies, architecture and solutions for different size and complexity. Our dexterous team of engineers are experts in troubleshooting of PLC/DCS system, PLC/DCS logic program and SCADA system troubleshooting. We have implemented discrete, continuous and batch process control across numerous industry sectors.

    SITE COMMISSONING:

    DigitalControls dedicated on-site team works around the clock to commission your installed or upgraded systems. We help to streamline your process & ensure that our systems run at optimum level.

  • Our domain expertise and capabilities include:
  • Pre-checking of panel before its on and checking power source within the panel i.e. earthling
  • Pre-commission activity (Panel IO testing, loop checking from panel as per the IO list)
  • Commissioning as per the process description (On-site modification requirement in SCADA& Logic as per sites requirement
  • Project Handover Reports

    • ANNUAL MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

    AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) ensures timely preventive maintenance of customers valuable test equipment. Our engineers will pre-empt problems, ensure complete repairing and quick redressal of complaints

    1. Conduct routine checks of Installed drives, PLC hardware and HMI software
    2. Conduct regular and virus checks on existing SCADA machines
    3. Check Panel Wiring, Power Supplies, Earth Pits and PF panel.
    4. Minor rectifications are carried out when required
